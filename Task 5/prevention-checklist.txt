Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
- Always verify payment requests before approving
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication on apps
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Check app authenticity before downloading

UPI Safety:
- Never approve 'collect requests' without verifying sender
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money

Report Cybercrime:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
