Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

DO's:
- Think before posting anything online
- Maintain privacy settings on your accounts
- Use respectful and professional language
- Verify information before sharing
- Build a positive digital footprint

DON'Ts:
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
- Don’t spread misinformation or fake news
- Don’t ignore digital etiquette
